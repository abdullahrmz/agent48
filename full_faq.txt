Voice Search / AI Marketing
What is voice search marketing?
Voice Search Marketing is the process of optimizing your digital content so it ranks in voice-based searches made via assistants like Siri, Alexa, or Google Assistant.
Why is it important?
More users are searching using voice — especially on mobile — and being voice-ready gives your brand an edge in discoverability.
